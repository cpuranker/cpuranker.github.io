About

CPURanker - the largest database of Android mobile CPU and GPU, with specifications, Antutu and GFXBench ratings.
Data is aggregated from the following websites: nanoreview, antutu, qualcomm, mediatek and others.

About me

I'm Aleksandr, QA Lead in game development.
This database was initially created to simplify device management in Google Play Console but eventually grew into a small pet project.
