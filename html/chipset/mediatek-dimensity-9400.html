MediaTek Dimensity 9400

The MediaTek Dimensity 9400 is an 8-core flagship mobile processor announced in October 2024. It is manufactured using a 3nm process technology. The chip features 1x Cortex-X925 at 3.62 GHz, 3x Cortex-X4 at 3.30 GHz, and 4x Cortex-A720 at 2.40 GHz.
