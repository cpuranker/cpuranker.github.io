Samsung Exynos 2500

The Samsung Exynos 2500 is a 10-core flagship mobile processor announced in June 2025. It is built using a 3nm manufacturing process. The chipset features 1x Cortex-X925 at 3.30 GHz, 2x Cortex-A725 at 2.74 GHz, 5x Cortex-A725 at 2.36 GHz, and 2x Cortex-A520 at 1.80 GHz.
