Google Tensor G5

The Google Tensor G5 is an 8-core mobile processor announced in August 2025. It is built using a 3nm manufacturing process. The chipset features 1x Cortex-X4 at 3.78 GHz, 5x Cortex-A725 at 3.05 GHz, and 2x Cortex-A520 at 2.25 GHz.
